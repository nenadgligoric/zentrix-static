strategy for proposal development

Step 1

Project idea and concept

We created an concept to develop proposal for a specific call

Step 2

Consortium building

Partners gathering and consortium building with dedicated roles matched to a call requirements. 

Step 3

Definition of the contribution

Definition of the roles and tasks, including the core scientific and technical work to be done.

Step 4

Drafting

Drafting the core sections of the proposal and meeting with dedicated groups, WP and task leaders.

FAQs

What is Horizon Europe

Horizon Europe is the ninth framework program of the European Union (EU) for research and innovation. It has a total budget of €95.5 billion for the period 2021-2027 and is aimed at supporting world-class research and innovation in Europe. The program aims to reinforce the EU’s position as a science and technology leader and drive economic growth by funding research and innovation activities in a wide range of fields, including health, digital, energy, environment, transportation, and more recently, blockchain and decentralized technologies. Horizon Europe also seeks to promote international cooperation and strengthen the relationship between research and industry. By funding cutting-edge research and innovation projects, Horizon Europe aims to address some of the biggest challenges facing society and deliver solutions that will improve people’s lives and create new economic opportunities. In line with this, Horizon Europe will also support the development and application of blockchain and decentralized technologies to address a wide range of challenges and create new opportunities in areas such as digital identity, secure data sharing, and secure, transparent and efficient supply chains, among others.

How can I apply?

To apply for funding through Horizon Europe, you will need to submit a proposal for a research and innovation project that aligns with the program’s goals and objectives.

How you can help me participate?

Our company is widening the network of partners that cooperate in Horizon funding. If you are interested to participate please contact us!

What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a type of digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security and operates independently of a central bank. Cryptocurrencies are decentralized, meaning that they are not issued or controlled by any government or financial institution. Instead, transactions are recorded on a distributed ledger called a blockchain, which is maintained by a network of computers around the world.

How does cryptocurrency wallet work?

To create a cryptocurrency wallet, you will need to choose a wallet provider and create an account. You will be given a unique public address and a private key that are associated with your wallet. The public address is like your bank account number, and it is what you will use to receive cryptocurrencies from others. The private key, on the other hand, is like your password, and it must be kept secret and secure, as it gives you access to your coins.
